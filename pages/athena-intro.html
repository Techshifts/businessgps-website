Understanding Your Results

Athena scores each step using a compounding model. Earlier weaknesses cascade downstream -- just like in real transformations.

Green (13-15)

Strong Foundation

You have clear capability in this area. Athena will show you how to maintain and build on this strength.

Amber (9-12)

Development Needed

Gaps exist that will affect downstream steps. Athena provides specific "Getting to Green" actions.

Red (1-8)

Critical Gap

This area needs attention before later steps can succeed. Athena prioritises what to fix first and why.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is Athena different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a general-purpose AI. Athena is transformation-specific: 45 data points, 380+ questions, a proven methodology, and persistent memory of your context. The analogy: ChatGPT is a brilliant generalist who just met you. Athena is a transformation specialist who knows your history.

How long does the assessment take?

Most executives complete the full 9-step assessment in 45-60 minutes. You can stop and resume at any point -- Athena remembers where you left off.

Is my data private?

Completely. Athena runs inside your own ChatGPT account. Only you can see your conversations, scores, and Success Map. Nothing is shared with anyone unless you choose to share it.

What do I need to prepare?

Have your role, industry, main transformation goal, and biggest concern ready. If you have strategy documents or consultant proposals, you can upload them for Athena to incorporate into your assessment.

Can I export my results?

Yes. After completing your assessment, ask Athena to export your Success Map to Excel. You'll get a structured spreadsheet with all scores, rationales, and improvement actions.

What happens after the assessment?

You'll have a scored Success Map showing exactly where you're strong and where gaps exist. From there, you can use Athena's advanced prompts to go deeper, invite team members for their own assessments, or join Start Right-30 for facilitated team alignment.
