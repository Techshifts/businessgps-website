Our Mission: Transformation Capability Management

We believe transformation should build permanent capability inside your organisation — not permanent dependency on external consultants.

BusinessGPS.ai exists because of a simple observation: the consulting industry has a $3.9 trillion incentive to keep you dependent. Every engagement that ends with "we'll need to come back for the next phase" is working as designed — for them.

We're building the opposite: a platform that makes your organisation smarter with every step, until you don't need us at all.

Our approach — Transformation Capability Management (TCM) — combines a battle-tested 45-step methodology with AI-powered guidance to give leaders the strategic clarity, operational confidence, and execution intelligence that used to require a team of expensive consultants.
