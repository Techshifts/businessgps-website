1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function properly and provide information to the site owner about how visitors use the site.

2. Cookies We Use

2.3 Cookies We Do Not Use

We do not use:

  • Advertising or tracking cookies
  • Third-party social media cookies
  • Cross-site tracking cookies

3. How to Manage Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Here's how to manage them in common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent certain features (such as checkout) from working correctly.

4. Google Analytics Opt-Out

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
