Strategic Clarity Check

Strategy 
Review our current transformation goals and answer:
1) What are we trying to achieve in the next 90 days?
2) What is the single biggest constraint preventing progress?
3) What would change if we removed that constraint?
4) Who needs to be involved in removing it?

Readiness Assessment Prep

Assessment 
Before running the Athena assessment, gather these inputs:
1) Current state description for each business area in scope
2) List of active transformation initiatives
3) Known blockers and dependencies
4) Team capacity and availability for the next quarter

Team Alignment Session

Team 
Run this alignment exercise with your leadership team:
1) Each person writes their top 3 transformation priorities silently
2) Share and compare — look for gaps, not agreement
3) Identify where priorities conflict
4) Agree on ONE priority that everyone supports for the next 30 days

Operational Constraint Map

Operations 
Map your operational constraints:
1) List every handoff point in your core delivery process
2) Identify where work queues up (WIP > capacity)
3) Measure the wait time at each queue
4) The longest queue is your constraint — everything else subordinates to it
