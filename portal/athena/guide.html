Getting Started with Athena

Athena is your readiness assessment tool. It analyses the current state of your transformation across five dimensions, giving you a clear picture of where you stand and what to do next.

How Athena Works

  1. Answer the readiness questions

    Athena asks structured questions about your transformation state. These cover strategy, leadership, capability, execution, and operations.

  2. Receive your assessment

    A Signal Path analysis showing clarity, constraints, collisions, leverage, and readiness. Each dimension gets a score and actionable commentary.

  3. Review with your team

    Walk through findings together and identify priorities. The assessment is designed to create shared understanding, not just a report.

  4. Take action

    Use the Getting to Green recommendations to address gaps. Each red or amber area includes specific next steps.

Tips for Best Results

  • Be honest — the assessment only helps if it reflects reality
  • Involve multiple perspectives — different roles see different constraints
  • Focus on what's blocking progress, not what's working fine
  • Revisit quarterly — your readiness changes as you progress
